About Me

My name is Aweke Babey. I was born in 1996 E.C. in G/Wuha. I have two brothers and one sister. I attended G/Wuha Primary School for my primary education and G/Wuha Secondary School for my secondary education. After achieving good results in my entrance exams, I joined Dire Dawa University, where I am currently studying Information Technology at the School of Computing.

Growing up in G/Wuha, I developed a strong interest in technology and computing. This passion led me to pursue a degree in Information Technology, where I am learning about various aspects of computing, including programming, networking, and data management. My goal is to become a skilled IT professional and contribute to the technological advancement of my community.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I enjoy spending time with my family and exploring new technologies. I am always eager to learn and take on new challenges, and I believe that continuous learning is key to personal and professional growth.
