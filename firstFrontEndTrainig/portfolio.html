PROJECT

Project Title:

EthioTelecom Internet Service Expansion

Opportunity

EthioTelecom has a significant opportunity to expand its internet services across Ethiopia. With the increasing demand for reliable and high-speed internet, especially in rural and underserved areas, EthioTelecom can play a crucial role in bridging the digital divide. The expansion will not only enhance connectivity but also support economic growth, education, and access to information.

Objective

The primary objective of the EthioTelecom Internet Service Expansion project is to provide reliable and affordable internet services to all regions of Ethiopia. This includes: Expanding network coverage to reach remote and rural areas. Improving the quality and speed of internet services. Introducing new technologies to enhance communication capabilities. Ensuring affordability to increase accessibility for all citizens1.

Main Goal

The main goal of this project is to become the leading digital solutions provider in Ethiopia by offering comprehensive internet services that meet the needs of individuals, businesses, and government entities. This involves: Increasing the subscriber base and market share. Enhancing customer satisfaction through superior service quality. Supporting digital transformation initiatives in various sectors2.

Additional Information

Strategic Initiatives: The project will focus on several strategic initiatives, including the deployment of 4G and 5G networks, expansion of fixed broadband services, and the introduction of innovative digital solutions. Economic Impact: By improving internet access, the project aims to stimulate economic activities, create job opportunities, and support the growth of small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). Social Impact: Enhanced internet services will facilitate better access to education, healthcare, and government services, contributing to overall social development. Sustainability: EthioTelecom is committed to sustainable practices, ensuring that the expansion project is environmentally friendly and socially responsible32.

This project represents a significant step towards a more connected and digitally empowered Ethiopia
